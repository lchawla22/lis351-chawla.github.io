Things I like

There's a lot of things I like. My most favorite thing is crocheting and looking at pretty views. I've been crocheting for about 10 years now and I've made plenty of objects. My most recent project was a sweater that I made. I've also made scarves and hats. Admiring pretty views has always been a favorite thing of mine. I like to capture the moment as best as possible, whenever I can.
